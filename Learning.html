Services and Tools for Language Learning and Teaching

LiveLanguage Games

A wide range of word games for first-language and second-language learners, children and adults, on general or specialised vocabularies. The lexical content of the games is generated automatically in the language(s) requested, and is adapted to the needs of the students.

Type: online applications.

Status: planned.

Bilingual Dictionaries

Use the lexical content of the UKC through a bilingual dictionary service, translating words from any language to any language. Currently, the service is accessible through a user interface. In the future, API-based access will also be provided.

Type: online service.

Status: released.

Available at: http://ukc.datascientia.eu/translate.
